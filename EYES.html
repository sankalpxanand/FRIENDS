OHHHHHHH MY EYES ! MY EYESSSSSSSSS !!

Friends usage:

Phoebe, when she found that her two of bestfriends were DOING IT.

How to use it now:

Now, I know that you know what eyes are. But apart from the usual expression such as ‘Oh My God’ or ‘Wow’, when you get shocked by something, you can use Phoebe’s way of coping with the shocking news.
