THEY DON'T KNOW WE KNOW THEY KNOW WE KNOW

Friends usage:

Phoebe and Rachel playing games as Monica and Chandler try to keep their relationship secret

How to use it now:

Every friendship group has its secrets. Alright, you might not get as deep as Rachel and Phoebe did, but there will 100% guaranteed be a time when you can utter the glorious words "they don't know we know", and it will add that extra sheen to the glimmer of scandal. If you ever do get to "they don't know we know they know we know", that is a life achievement unlocked.
